Crescent Lake Challenge

Sunday February 23rd, 2025

CLC is a 23 kilometer free style cross country ski race loop around Crescent Lake.

Event Details

About the Race
Crescent Lake Challenge is a 23 kilometer loop around Crescent Lake, Oregon. The race begins at 10:00 AM Pacific Time at the Crescent Lake Sno-Park (Sno-Park Permit required). Entry Fees Pre-Race
Open Division $45
21 and under $25
​
​Entry Fees day of race
Open Division $65
21 and Under $45 If the event is cancelled for any reason race entry fees will be refunded.
​
​Race entry includes SportHill item for each entrant, hot lunch (vegi and non-vegi options) and raffle prize drawings.

Prize money will be awarded to the top 3 men and women finishers.
We will have a Soft Start at 9am for skiers who need extra time. However, Soft Start skiers will not be eligible for overall awards and age group awards. Please let us know at bib pick-up and check in if you are doing the Soft Start.

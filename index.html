Crescent Lake Challenge

TextMarch 5, 2023 in Crescent Lake, OR
CLC is a 23 kilometer free style cross country ski race loop around Crescent Lake.

Event Details

About the Race
Crescent Lake Challenge is a 23 kilometer loop around Crescent Lake, Oregon. The race begins at 10:00 AM Pacific Time at the Crescent Lake Sno-Park (Sno-Park Permit required).Entry Fees Pre-Race
Open Division $40
21 and under $20

Entry Fees day of race
Open Division $60
21 and Under $30If the event is cancelled for any reason race entry fees will be refunded.

Race entry includes SportHill item for each entrant, hot lunch (vegi and non-vegi options) and raffle prize drawings.

Prize money will be awarded to the top 3 men and women finishers.

My wife and I started the Crescent Lake Challenge because we love the terrain and views of the lake and local peaks that the ski race course provides. The distance of 23 kilometers is long enough to be challenging but not so long that a fairly new skier can't accomplish. It's also the only race in Oregon that loops around a lake.

- Jim Hill
